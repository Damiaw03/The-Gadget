PAYMENT METHOD


Online Banking

Online banking is a digital service provided by banks that allows customers to
manage their accounts and conduct financial transactions over the internet.
The gadget has been using a online banking as a payment method in
business transactions between customer and seller




Cash On Deliver (COD)

Cash on Delivery (COD) is a payment method where customers pay for goods at the time of delivery rather than in advance. This option allows buyers to inspect the product before making the payment, providing a sense of
security and reducing the risk of fraud. The gadget allowed the customer to use a COD method but,
we will charge a deposit for the customer who apply this method and get their personal data.
This is because we need to avoid from being scam by improper people.





PayLatter

PayLater is a payment option that allows consumers to purchase and
pay for them at a later date, either through deferred payments
or installments. Our organization accepted a PayLetter
payment method,but we just accept PayLetter from
Shopee and gopay only.
